Who Am I?

I am a computer science student currently studying in an undergraduate course. I love programming. This is the reason I chose the field of computer science and engineering. To be specific why I love computers so much, I believe it is due to the fact I am enamoured by the capabilities of computers and how they have achieved many marvelous feats. I am also interested in digital electronics as they deal with how the computer operates at hardware level and I am also interested in the logic behind it. The size of the transistors keeps reducing, and now we are at 7nm with latest Ryzen 5 3600 and other Ryzen Gen 3 products. This ability of ours to shrink the size of the transistors to such an extend amuses me to no end.
